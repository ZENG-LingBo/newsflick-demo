US–Iran conflict · Day 12

The strait that prices the world is closed

Twelve days of strikes have hit more than 5,000 targets across Iran, and the response has shut the Strait of Hormuz — the channel a fifth of the world's oil passes through.

Officials describe the campaign as pressure. What no briefing has offered is any description of how it ends.

The stated objective has shifted from nuclear sites to "restoring deterrence".
