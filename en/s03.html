US–Canada trade · Effective this week

50% tariffs, between allies

The order is signed: 50% tariffs on most Canadian goods, citing autos, alcohol and dairy. It is the steepest tariff wall between two allied economies in modern history.

Ottawa calls it "economic coercion" and is drafting retaliation. The price effects will not wait for the politics.

The case each side makes…

The Arguments

For the tariffs

Canada has "unfairly discriminated" against US autos, alcohol and dairy for years.

Leverage now forces a better deal faster than another decade of panels.

Against

A tax on your closest supply chain is a tax on your own factories.

Households pay first: an estimated $2,400 a year per US family.

Both sides accept one thing: integrated industries cannot re-shore in a single season.
