Hong Kong · Rental market

The steepest summer climb in a decade

Hong Kong rents rose almost 4% in the first half — on pace for the biggest summer gain since 2016. The drivers are measurable: a steady inflow of mainland professionals and students, returning expat demand, and thin supply.

Whether this is recovery or squeeze depends on which side of a lease you sit.
